About Me

I'm a user experience researcher and designer. I'm currently a PhD student in Human-Computer Interaction at Stanford
University, where I work with James Landay and other amazing people in the HCI Group. My research focuses on
understanding how people interact with AI systems, with the goal of designing more transparent, trustworthy, and
equitable AI technologies. Before coming to Stanford, I worked as a UX designer and researcher at various tech
companies and labs, including Google, Airbnb, and the MIT Media Lab. I'm passionate about diversity and
inclusion in tech, and have led several initiatives to promote equity and representation for underrepresented groups in
the industry.
